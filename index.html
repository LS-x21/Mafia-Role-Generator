Web Navigation

At the home page, click start to begin choosing your roles. Then look at diagrams to see what does what. If on mobile tap on image to expand, tap elsewhere to return to normal size.

Roles

Villager The innocent people who have no special powers.
Prophet Can check one person’s identity each night. The judge will reveal that person’s identity as “good” or “bad” only to the Prophet.
Guard Can protect one person from the Werewolves each night. The protector can also protect themselves if needed. They cannot protect the same person two nights in a row. Protected people are not safe from being voted out.
Witch Can heal and poison one target only once throughout the whole game. Each night, the Witch will know who died (before their healing power is used) and can choose to save them or not. They may only save themselves the first night if killed.
Hunter Upon death, they can shoot down a person with them if wanted. If they are poisoned by the Witch, they may not use their power.
Idiot Can’t die when voted out, but will lose their power to vote. They will still be involved during daytime conversations. If killed for other reasons, they will die.
Wolf Can communicate with their teammates to kill one person each night. Their objective is to kill all the innocents and try to hide their identities. They can kill one of their own members during the night, but this won’t be counted as a “suicide”.
White Wolf King Communicates with their teammates to kill one person each night with the other Werewolves. Their special ability is when they suicide during the day, they may choose to drag one person down with them.
Pretty Wolf Can also kill one person each night with the other Werewolves. They can choose a person as a hostage during the night for each night and until the following day. If the Pretty Wolf dies, the person who they held hostage will also die. They may not choose the same hostage person for two nights in a row, and the Pretty Wolf can’t suicide
Cupid Can link any two people together during the first night of the game (which is also their only chance of doing so). If one of the linked people is killed, the other one dies as well.
Bomb Master If they are voted out during the day, EVERYONE who voted them dies with them as well.
Elder When killed by the Werewolves for the first time, they will not die, but the second time they were killed they do die. If killed for other reasons, they will immediately die.
Penguin Can freeze one person each night, and that person loses the ability to use any of their power for that night and the following day (the Penguin may choose to freeze themselves if needed). Werewolves can still suicide during the day or kill targets in the night if frozen.
Bear Each night the Bear will private message the judge any four players’ names. After each night, the judge will announce whether the Bear roared. If the Bear roared, then at least 2 out of the 4 people the Bear checked are Werewolves. Other people will not know who the Bear is and who got checked.
Sheep The Sheep is a spy for the Werewolves. They will not be in the Werewolf group chat and the Werewolves will not know them. Whenever the prophet or the Bear checks their identity, it will appear as “good”. The Sheep have one chance to join the Werewolf group chat and meet their Werewolf teammates. Anytime during the night, they can tell the judge a person (either alive or dead) what they think is a Werewolf. If they got it correct, they will be invited to the chat, and their identity will be turned out as “bad” if checked. If they guessed wrong, then they would be announced dead the next morning. The Sheep’s winning condition is the same as the Werewolves. Sheep cannot Suicide.
Rooster If killed during the night by the Werewolves, there will be no daytime the following day. The game will immediately go into the next night. If killed for any other reason or frozen by the Penguin, there will be daytime after the night but the Rooster still dies.
